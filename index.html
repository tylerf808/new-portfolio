Who I am

Full Stack Web Developer Based In Baltimore

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Tyler Fabian and I have been interesting in computer programming and web development since my highschool days. I started with learning basic and advanced object oriented programming in java and eventually branched out into javascript, python, and other languages. I've recently graduated from the Johns Hopkins School of Engineering coding boot camp where I learned full stack web development. During this course I learned dozens of technologies and worked with my fellow students to complete a wide range of projects, many of which can be viewed below in the my work section.

When I'm not coding I'm usually busy playing guitar or working on remastering historical footage. My preferred guitar style is improvised jam band and I love jamming out over some Grateful Dead backing tracks. As for remastering historical footage, I have a fascination with the first world war and I have been using AI software to enhance rare footage from the war that I have found. I hope that in upscaling, colorizing, and frame rate boosting I can create videos that humanize those people from over a century ago.

I am equally passionate about coding and web development as I am with my hobbies, and I hope to be working on some grate projects in the future. Please reach out to me in the contact section if you are interested in my skills.
